<p><p>Medical Device Cooling Equipment market offers two main types of cooling systems: liquid-based and air-based. Liquid-based cooling systems use a fluid such as water or refrigerant to absorb heat from the medical devices, keeping them at stable operating temperatures. On the other hand, air-based cooling systems use circulating air to dissipate heat generated by the devices. Both types of cooling systems play a crucial role in maintaining the functionality and longevity of medical devices by preventing overheating and ensuring optimal performance.</p></p>
