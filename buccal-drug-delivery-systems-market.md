<p><p>Buccal drug delivery systems refer to a method of delivering drugs through the buccal mucosa, which is the inner lining of the cheek. This route of drug administration offers several advantages, including easy administration, avoidance of first-pass metabolism, and improved patient compliance. Buccal drug delivery systems are especially suitable for drugs that have poor oral bioavailability and those that require sustained release for a localized effect.</p><p>The global buccal drug delivery systems market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The market is driven by factors such as the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, rising geriatric population, and growing demand for efficient drug delivery systems. Additionally, advancements in technology and the development of innovative buccal drug delivery systems are further boosting market growth.</p><p>The market is witnessing various trends that are shaping its landscape. One of the notable trends is the increasing adoption of mucoadhesive polymers in buccal drug delivery systems. These polymers adhere to the buccal mucosa, allowing for prolonged drug release and enhanced bioavailability. Another trend includes the use of nanotechnology for the development of buccal drug delivery systems, enabling targeted and controlled drug release.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has also had a positive impact on the market, with an increased focus on the development of buccal drug delivery systems for antiviral drugs and vaccines.
