<p><p>The medical dry electrodes market encompasses two main types: disposable and reusable electrodes. Disposable electrodes are designed for single-use and are discarded after each use, ensuring hygiene and reducing the risk of contamination. They are commonly used in short-term monitoring applications. In contrast, reusable electrodes are made from durable materials and can be used multiple times after proper cleaning and maintenance. Reusable electrodes are often used in long-term monitoring applications, making them more cost-effective in the long run. Both types play a crucial role in various medical settings, offering flexibility and convenience for different monitoring needs.</p></p>
