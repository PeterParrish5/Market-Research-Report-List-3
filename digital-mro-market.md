Robotics refers to the use of automated machines and robots to perform maintenance and repair tasks, increasing efficiency and reducing costs. Big Data Analytics involves analyzing large volumes of data to gain insights and improve operational efficiency in MRO. Digital Twin is a virtual replica of physical assets that can be used for simulation, monitoring, and predictive maintenance purposes.</p></p>
