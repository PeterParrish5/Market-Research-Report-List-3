<p><strong>The Polymer Material Light Stabilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free Radical Scavengers</li><li>UV Absorber</li><li>Quencher</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymer Material Light Stabilizers are used to protect polymers from degradation caused by UV radiation. The market is segmented into different types such as Free Radical Scavengers, UV Absorbers, Quenchers, and Others. Free Radical Scavengers work by neutralizing radicals generated by UV exposure. UV Absorbers absorb and dissipate UV radiation. Quenchers deactivate excited states of molecules caused by UV light. Other types of stabilizers may include hindered amine light stabilizers (HALS) and benzophenones, which offer different mechanisms of protection against UV degradation.</p></p>
