<p><p>The market for capillary stick needles can be classified into two main types: safety lancets and ordinary lancets. Safety lancets are designed to prevent accidental needlestick injuries by automatically retraction of the needle after use. They are widely used in healthcare settings to collect blood samples. On the other hand, ordinary lancets are simple, disposable needles used for blood sampling by pricking the skin. They lack the safety features of safety lancets but are commonly used by individuals for self-monitoring purposes, such as glucose testing for diabetes.</p></p>
