<p><p>Honeycomb core packaging is available in three main types: continuous unexpanded, slices unexpanded, and pre-expanded sheets. Continuous unexpanded honeycomb core packaging is in the form of a continuous roll, allowing for customization of length and application in various industries. Slices unexpanded honeycomb core packaging is pre-cut into smaller pieces for specific packaging needs. Pre-expanded sheets are larger sheets of honeycomb core that have already been expanded, making them ready for immediate use. Each type offers unique benefits for different packaging requirements.</p></p>
