<p><p>The Non Invasive Colon Cancer Screening Market comprises two popular types of tests - the Fecal Immunochemical Test (FIT) and the Guaiac-based Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT). FIT measures blood in the stool using antibodies, providing higher accuracy and sensitivity compared to gFOBT. On the other hand, gFOBT detects hidden blood in the stool by a chemical reaction. FIT has gained more popularity as its results are not influenced by dietary restrictions, making it more convenient and reliable for patients. Both tests aid in early detection of colon cancer, promoting timely treatment and improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
