<p><p>The mobile CRM software market can be classified into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based mobile CRM software operates on remote servers, enabling users to access the software and data through the internet. It offers scalability, flexibility, and ease of implementation but requires continuous internet connectivity. On the other hand, on-premises mobile CRM software is hosted on local servers, providing users with complete control and privacy over their data. However, it requires substantial upfront investment, maintenance, and technical expertise for deployment and management.</p></p>
