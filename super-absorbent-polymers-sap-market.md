Sodium polyacrylate is widely used for its high absorbency and gel-forming properties. Polysaccharides are natural polymers derived from plants and are biodegradable. Polyacrylamide copolymer is known for its water retention capabilities. Polylactic acid (PLA) is a biodegradable polymer, and Polyvinyl alcohol is used for its strong binding properties.</p></p>
