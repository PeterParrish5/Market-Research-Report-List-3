<p><p>Peristaltic pumps are categorized into four main types: Variable Speed (Metering) Type, Flow Control Type, Dispensing (Dosing) Type, and Transfer Type. </p><p>Variable Speed (Metering) Type pumps are used for precise metering of fluids by controlling the speed of the pump. Flow Control Type pumps regulate the flow rate of fluids in pipelines. Dispensing (Dosing) Type pumps are used for accurate dosing of fluids in various applications. Transfer Type pumps are designed for transferring fluids from one location to another with high efficiency and minimal risk of contamination.</p></p>
