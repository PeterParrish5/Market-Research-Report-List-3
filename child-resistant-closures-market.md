<p><p>Child resistant closures in the market are available in two main types: reclosable and non-reclosable. Non-reclosable closures, on the other hand, are intended for one-time use only and cannot be resealed after opening. Both types of closures serve the important function of protecting children from accessing potentially harmful substances while still allowing for easy access for adults.</p></p>
