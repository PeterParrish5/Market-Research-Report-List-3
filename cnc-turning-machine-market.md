<p><strong>The CNC Turning Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spindle Orientation: Horizontal</li><li>Spindle Orientation: Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CNC turning machines can be classified based on spindle orientation as horizontal or vertical. Horizontal turning machines have the spindle mounted parallel to the ground, ideal for heavy cutting and high production rates. On the other hand, vertical turning machines have the spindle mounted perpendicular to the ground, allowing for easier chip evacuation and better tool access. Both types are widely used in various industries for precision turning operations, but the choice between them depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
