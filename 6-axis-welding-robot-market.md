<p><p>The 6-axis welding robot market includes spot welding robots and arc welding robots. Spot welding robots are capable of joining two metal pieces together by applying heat and pressure on specific spots. Arc welding robots, on the other hand, use a high-intensity electric arc to melt and fuse metals. Both types of robots bring automation and precision to the welding process, offering higher efficiency and improved quality. These robots are widely used in industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and construction for their ability to perform complex welding tasks with minimal human intervention.</p></p>
