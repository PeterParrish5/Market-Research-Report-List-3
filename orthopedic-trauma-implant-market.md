<p><p>The orthopedic trauma implant market consists of various types of implants used in the treatment of traumatic bone injuries. This includes plates, screws, interlocking nails, and other implants. Plates are used to stabilize fractures by holding the bone fragments in place, while screws are used to fixate plates or directly into the bone. Interlocking nails are inserted into the hollow center of long bones to provide stability and support. Other implants may include wires, pins, staples, and external fixation devices.</p></p>
