<p><strong>The Polyimide Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless Tubing</li><li>Spiral Wound Tubing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyimide Tube market comprises two main types of tubes: Seamless Tubing and Spiral Wound Tubing. Seamless Tubing is manufactured without any seams or welds, providing superior strength and resistance to high temperatures. On the other hand, Spiral Wound Tubing is made by winding a strip of polyimide around a mandrel, creating a tube with high flexibility and excellent chemical resistance. Both types of tubes are widely used in industries such as aerospace, electronics, and automotive for various applications requiring high performance under extreme conditions.</p></p>
