<p><p>Bicycle insurance market includes three main types of policies. Third-Party Insurance Policy provides coverage for damages or injuries caused to a third party while using the insured bicycle. Standalone own-damage Insurance Policy covers damages to the insured bicycle due to accidents, theft, or fire. Comprehensive Insurance Policy combines both third-party coverage and own-damage coverage, providing a wider range of protection. These market types cater to different needs and budgets of bicycle owners, allowing them to choose the level of coverage that suits them best.</p></p>
