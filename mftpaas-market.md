<p><p>MFTPaaS, or Managed File Transfer Platform-as-a-Service, encompasses three market types. People-Centric File Transfer emphasizes ease of use and collaboration, enabling individuals to securely share files within and outside an organization. On the other hand, Extreme File Transfer market caters to businesses requiring ultra-fast, large file transfers typically for data-intensive industries like media, research, or manufacturing. These distinct market types within MFTPaaS address specific needs related to file transfer in different organizational contexts.</p></p>
