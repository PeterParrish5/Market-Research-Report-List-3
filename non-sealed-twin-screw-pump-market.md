<p><p>Non-sealed Twin-Screw Pump Market Types include Single Volute Twin Screw Pump and Double Volutes Twin Screw Pump. Single Volute Twin Screw Pumps have a single volute casing which allows for the movement of fluid through the pump in a more efficient manner. On the other hand, Double Volutes Twin Screw Pumps have two volute casings which help in balancing the axial forces and reducing vibrations, resulting in smoother operation and longer pump lifespan. Both types are used in various industries for efficient fluid transfer.</p></p>
