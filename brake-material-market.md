Brake pads are commonly used in disc brakes and are pressed against the rotor to create friction. Brake shoes are used in drum brakes to press against the inside of the drum. Other types of brake materials may include brake discs, rotors, and calipers. Each type of brake material serves a specific purpose in the braking system of a vehicle.</p></p>
