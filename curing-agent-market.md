<p><p>Curing agents are essential components in various industries such as coatings, adhesives, and composites. Epoxy curing agents are widely used due to their excellent adhesion and chemical resistance. Amides are commonly used in adhesives and sealants for their high temperature and humidity resistance. Silanes are utilized in construction materials for enhancing durability and adhesion. Isocyanates are popular in the automotive industry for their fast curing and flexibility. Arizidine curing agents are known for their high chemical and temperature resistance. Other curing agents include phenolic resins and polyurethane catalysts.</p></p>
