<p><p>Peppermint oil is available in two primary market types: natural and synthetic. Natural peppermint oil is extracted from the peppermint plant through steam distillation, offering a pure and organic option. On the other hand, synthetic peppermint oil is created in a laboratory using artificial ingredients to mimic the scent and properties of natural peppermint oil. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with natural oil being favored by those seeking pure, authentic products, while synthetic oil offers a more cost-effective alternative.</p></p>
