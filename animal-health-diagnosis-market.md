Urinalysis involves evaluating urine samples to detect urinary tract infections or kidney diseases. Immunodiagnostics uses techniques like ELISA to detect antibodies or antigens in animal samples. Hematology examines blood cells to detect and monitor diseases such as anemia or infections. Molecular Diagnostics combines genetics and molecular biology to identify DNA or RNA markers to diagnose diseases.</p></p>
