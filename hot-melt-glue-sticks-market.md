They are often preferred for arts and crafts projects or when working with light-colored materials. Opaque glue sticks, on the other hand, are solid in color and are typically used for applications where a more discrete bond is needed, such as in packaging or construction. Both types offer strong bonding capabilities and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
