<p><p>Web hosting services can be categorized into two main types, namely Cloud Based and On-Premise. Cloud-based hosting refers to a service where websites are hosted on servers located in remote data centers. This allows for scalability, flexibility, and easy remote access. On the other hand, On-Premise hosting involves hosting websites on servers within a physical location, such as an organization's own data center. This type of hosting provides complete control over the infrastructure but may require more resources for maintenance and security.</p></p>
