<p><strong>The Medical Radiation Shielding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Body Protection</li><li>Partial Protection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical radiation shielding market consists of two main types: full body protection and partial protection. Full body protection involves shielding the entire body from radiation exposure, typically using lead aprons or vests. This type of shielding is commonly used in radiology departments and during surgical procedures. On the other hand, partial protection focuses on shielding specific body parts or organs that are at higher risk of radiation exposure, such as the thyroid or the eyes. This type of shielding is often used in dental clinics or nuclear medicine facilities.</p></p>
