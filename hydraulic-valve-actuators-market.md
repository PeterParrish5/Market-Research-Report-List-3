<p><p>Hydraulic valve actuators are used to operate valves in various industrial applications. Linear actuators are designed to translate force in a straight line, suitable for applications where linear motion is required. Rotary actuators, on the other hand, convert fluid pressure into rotational motion, ideal for applications where valves need to be turned or rotated. Both types of actuators play a crucial role in controlling the flow of fluids and gases in hydraulic systems, ensuring efficient and precise operation of valves.</p></p>
