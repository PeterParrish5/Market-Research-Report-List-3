<p><p>Polymer Modified Bitumen (PMB) is a type of bitumen that has been enhanced with polymers to improve its performance and durability. There are different types of PMB, including SBS Modified Bitumen, Plastomer Modified Bitumen, and Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen. SBS Modified Bitumen utilizes styrene-butadiene-styrene polymers, while Plastomer Modified Bitumen incorporates plastomers for increased flexibility. Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen incorporates recycled rubber crumb, making it more resistant to cracking and aging. These different PMB types offer enhanced properties and are widely used in various road construction applications for longer-lasting and more resilient pavements.</p></p>
