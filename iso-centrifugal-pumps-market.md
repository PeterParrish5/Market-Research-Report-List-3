<p><p>Horizontal ISO Centrifugal Pumps are widely used in industries where floor space is limited. They are designed to handle high flow rates and are suitable for applications where the pump needs to be installed above the liquid level. On the other hand, Vertical ISO Centrifugal Pumps are commonly used when space constraints are not an issue. They are designed for applications where the liquid level is below the pump and are often used in industrial and municipal settings.</p></p>
