<p><p>Blockchain distributed ledger is a technology that allows multiple participants to maintain a shared digital record of transactions in a secure and transparent manner. This growth can be attributed to several factors, including the rising adoption of blockchain technology across various industries such as finance, supply chain, healthcare, and government sectors. <p><p>Private blockchain refers to a distributed ledger system that is operated and controlled by a single organization or a group of entities with restricted access.
