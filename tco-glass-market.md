<p><p>TCO glass, also known as transparent conductive oxide glass, is widely used in various industries for its unique properties. The three main types of TCO glass are ITO (Indium Tin Oxide), FTO (Fluorine Doped Tin Oxide), and AZO (Aluminum Doped Zinc Oxide). ITO is the most commonly used TCO glass due to its high conductivity and transparency. FTO is known for its lower cost and good durability. AZO offers a good balance between conductivity and cost, making it suitable for many applications.</p></p>
