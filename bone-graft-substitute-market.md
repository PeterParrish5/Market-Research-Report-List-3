<p><strong>The Bone Graft Substitute Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autografts</li><li>Allografts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bone graft substitutes market includes two main types - autografts and allografts. Autografts are bone grafts taken from the patient's own body, typically from the hip or the ribs. These are considered the gold standard for bone grafting due to their high success rate. Allografts, on the other hand, are bone grafts sourced from a donor, either living or deceased. These are widely used as they eliminate the need for a second surgical site and are readily available. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages in the bone graft substitute market.</p></p>
