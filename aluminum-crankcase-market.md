<p><p>The aluminum crankcase market is divided into two main types: the upper crankcase and the lower crankcase. The upper crankcase refers to the part of the engine that contains the cylinder head, valves, and camshaft. It provides protection and support to these components. On the other hand, the lower crankcase is the bottom section of the engine that houses the crankshaft and oil pan. It ensures the proper functioning and lubrication of the crankshaft, allowing it to rotate smoothly and efficiently. These two market segments cater to different engine components and functionalities within the aluminum crankcase industry.</p></p>
