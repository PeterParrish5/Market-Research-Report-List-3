<p><strong>The Medical Injection Bottles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mold-made Bottles</li><li>Tube-made Bottles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical injection bottles market comprises two main types of bottles: mold-made bottles and tube-made bottles. Mold-made bottles are produced using molds, which provide a precise shape and size to the bottle. These bottles are commonly used for sterile liquids and have a high demand due to their uniformity and consistent quality. On the other hand, tube-made bottles are created from extruded tubes that are cut to the desired length and sealed at one end. These bottles are more cost-effective and are frequently used for non-sterile liquids or medicines.</p></p>
