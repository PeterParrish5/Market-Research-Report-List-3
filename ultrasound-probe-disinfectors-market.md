<p><strong>The Ultrasound Probe Disinfectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intermediate/Low-Level Disinfection (LLDs/ILDs)</li><li>High-Level Disinfection (HLD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasound Probe Disinfectors Market includes Intermediate/Low-Level Disinfection (LLDs/ILDs) and High-Level Disinfection (HLD) types. LLDs/ILDs are typically used for routine disinfection of ultrasound probes, offering moderate efficacy against certain pathogens. On the other hand, HLD involves a more intensive disinfection process, effectively killing a broader range of pathogens. HLD is recommended for probes that come into contact with sterile body cavities or exhibit a high risk of transmitting infections, while LLDs/ILDs are suitable for general-purpose disinfection.</p></p>
