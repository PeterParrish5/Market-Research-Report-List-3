<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis Products & Supplies</li><li>Peritoneal Dialysis Products & Supplies</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dialysis equipment and services market includes two main types: hemodialysis products & supplies, and peritoneal dialysis products & supplies. Hemodialysis involves using a machine to filter waste and excess fluids from the blood, typically performed at a healthcare facility. Hemodialysis products & supplies include machines, catheters, tubing, and dialyzers. Peritoneal dialysis, on the other hand, involves using the body's own peritoneal membrane to filter waste and fluids. Peritoneal dialysis products & supplies include catheters, dialysis solution bags, and transfer sets. Both types of dialysis provide life-sustaining treatments for individuals with kidney failure.</p></p>
