<p><p>Light control films are available in two main types based on the substrate material used – PET substrate and non-PET substrate. PET substrate films are made from polyethylene terephthalate, offering high durability and flexibility. Non-PET substrate films are made from materials other than polyethylene terephthalate, such as polycarbonate or acrylic. Each type of substrate offers unique benefits and is suited for different applications based on factors like cost, optical properties, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
