<p><ul><li>Circulating Fluidized Bed (CFB)</li><li>Bubbling Fluidized Bed (BFB)</li><li>Dual Fluidized Bed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluidized bed gasifier is a technology used for converting solid fuels into gas. In the circulating fluidized bed (CFB) market, fuel particles are suspended and circulated in a high velocity fluidized bed, promoting efficient combustion and gasification. Bubbling fluidized bed (BFB) market operates at lower velocities, allowing for better control of temperature and gasification efficiency. Dual fluidized bed market involves two separate fluidized beds, with one gasifying the fuel and the other regenerating the gasification agent. These markets offer distinct advantages and are used in various applications for cleaner energy production.</p></p>
