<p><p>The 193nm photoresist market consists of two main types: dry and wet photoresists. Dry 193nm photoresists are chemically amplified resists that undergo a series of reactions upon exposure to 193nm light, resulting in a patterned layer on the substrate. On the other hand, wet 193nm photoresists are more traditional and rely on a solvent to develop the exposed areas. Both types offer high resolution and sensitivity, catering to different needs in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
