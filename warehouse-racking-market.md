<p><p>The warehouse racking market is comprised of several types of racks designed to optimize storage space in warehouses. Selective pallet rack allows easy access to each pallet, ensuring swift picking and stocking. Double deep rack provides higher density storage by placing pallets two deep, while push-back rack allows for increased storage capacity by utilizing a 'last-in, first-out' method. Drive-in rack maximizes storage density for bulk goods, as it enables forklifts to enter and retrieve from one side. Lastly, there are other types of racks available, offering different storage solutions based on warehouse needs.</p></p>
