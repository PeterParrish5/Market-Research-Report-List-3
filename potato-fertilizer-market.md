<p><p>The potato fertilizer market consists of two main types: organic fertilizer and compound fertilizer. Organic fertilizer is derived from natural sources, such as animal manure, compost, and plant residues. It helps improve soil fertility and provides essential nutrients to potatoes in a slow-release manner. On the other hand, compound fertilizer is a synthetic product made by combining different nutrients. It offers precise nutrient content and is easily absorbed by plants. Both types of fertilizers play a crucial role in improving potato yield and quality, but they differ in terms of composition and production methods.</p></p>
