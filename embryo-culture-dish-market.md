<p><p>Embryo culture dish market types include various options. Micro-droplet culture dish provides a controlled environment for embryo development in small droplets. The 5 well culture dish divides the space into five wells, allowing the growth of multiple embryos simultaneously. Centre well dish has a central compartment for the primary embryo, surrounded by a larger compartment for supporting cells. ICSI dish is specifically designed for intracytoplasmic sperm injection procedures. Additionally, other types are available, each offering unique features for specific embryo culture requirements.</p></p>
