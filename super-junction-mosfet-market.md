<p><p>Super-Junction MOSFET market types include Multiple-Epitaxy and Deep-Trench technologies. Multiple-Epitaxy technology involves the use of multiple epitaxial layers to enhance breakdown voltage and reduce specific on-resistance. On the other hand, Deep-Trench technology utilizes deep trenches in the silicon substrate to achieve a high breakdown voltage and low on-resistance. Both technologies aim to improve the performance of Super-Junction MOSFETs by optimizing the structure of the device and enhancing its overall efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
