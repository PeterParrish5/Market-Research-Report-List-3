<p><strong>The Stainless Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Welded</li><li>Seamless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stainless tube market is divided into two main types: welded and seamless tubes. Welded tubes are made by welding strips of steel together, resulting in a stronger and more cost-effective solution. On the other hand, seamless tubes are manufactured by extruding a solid billet through a piercing rod, creating a smooth and consistent product with no welds. Welded tubes are commonly used for structural applications, while seamless tubes are preferred for high-pressure and corrosive environments. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
