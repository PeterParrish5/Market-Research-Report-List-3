<p><p>Organic scintillators market consists of three main types: organic crystals, organic liquids, and plastic scintillators. Organic crystals are solid materials that emit light when excited by ionizing radiation. Organic liquids are liquid materials that also emit light when exposed to radiation. Plastic scintillators, on the other hand, are made of polymer matrices that contain scintillating compounds. Each type of scintillator has its advantages and disadvantages, making them suitable for different applications in radiation detection and imaging.</p></p>
