<p><p>Warehouse automation refers to the process of using technological systems to execute various tasks in a warehouse, leading to increased efficiency and productivity. Goods-To-Person Technologies (GTP) involve automated systems that retrieve items and deliver them to workers, eliminating the need for manual searching. Pick-To-Light Systems use light indicators to guide workers to the right items, reducing picking errors. Sortation Systems automate the process of sorting items for shipping. Collaborative Mobile Robots work alongside humans, aiding in tasks like picking and replenishment. Other market segments include automated conveyor systems, automated guided vehicles, and warehouse control software.</p></p>
