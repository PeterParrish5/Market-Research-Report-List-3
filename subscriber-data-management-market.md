<p><p>Subscriber Data Management (SDM) is a crucial part of telecommunications and network service providers' infrastructure. It involves managing and centralizing subscriber data for various purposes like authentication, billing, and service delivery. SDM encompasses two main market types: premise-based and cloud-based. </p><p>Premise-based SDM solutions are implemented and operated on the service providers' own infrastructure. They provide complete control and customization options but require significant upfront investment and maintenance costs. </p><p>On the other hand, cloud-based SDM solutions are hosted and operated on remote servers, accessed via the internet. They offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as service providers can pay for the resources they use without the need for extensive hardware or software investment.</p></p>
