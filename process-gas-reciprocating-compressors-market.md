<p><p>Process Gas Reciprocating Compressors come in two main types: Single Stage and Multi-Stage. Single Stage compressors have one cylinder that compresses the gas in a single stroke, making them simpler and more cost-effective for lower pressure applications. Multi-Stage compressors, on the other hand, have multiple cylinders that compress the gas in stages, allowing them to achieve higher levels of compression for more demanding applications. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the process gas being compressed.</p></p>
