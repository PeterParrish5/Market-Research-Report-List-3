<p><p>A marine propeller plug is a device used to prevent marine growth on the propeller of a boat or ship. The propeller plug is installed on the propeller shaft and releases anti-fouling agents to prevent marine growth.</p><p>The Marine Propeller Plug Market is experiencing steady growth due to the increasing demand for efficient propulsion systems in the marine industry.
