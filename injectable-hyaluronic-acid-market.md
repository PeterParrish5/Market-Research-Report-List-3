<p><p>The injectable hyaluronic acid market includes two types of products: single-phase and duplex. Single-phase products consist of hyaluronic acid alone and are used to add volume to specific areas of the face, such as lips or cheeks. Duplex products, on the other hand, combine hyaluronic acid with lidocaine, a local anesthetic. The lidocaine helps to numb the area, making the injection process more comfortable for the patient. Both single-phase and duplex products are designed to rejuvenate and enhance the appearance of the face by filling in wrinkles and restoring lost volume.</p></p>
