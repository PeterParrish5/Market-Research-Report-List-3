<p><p>Textile softeners include liquid type, dryer sheets, and dryer balls. Liquid type softeners are added to the washing machine during the rinse cycle to soften fabrics. Dryer sheets are placed in the dryer to reduce static electricity and add fragrance to clothes. Dryer balls are reusable balls that are placed in the dryer with laundry to reduce drying time and soften fabrics. Each type of textile softener offers unique benefits to consumers looking to achieve soft, fresh-smelling clothes.</p></p>
