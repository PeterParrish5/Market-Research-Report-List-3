Antimony Oxide is commonly used for its high efficiency in suppressing flames. Aluminium Trihydrate releases water vapor when exposed to heat, cooling down the material. Organophosphates inhibit combustion by forming a protective char layer. Boron Compounds release gases that dilute flammable gases. Other types of flame retardants offer unique properties to enhance fire safety in aerospace applications.</p></p>
