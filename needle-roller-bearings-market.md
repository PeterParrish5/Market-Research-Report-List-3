<p><p>Needle roller bearings are commonly categorized into two main types based on their design and structure: Single-Row Bearings and Double-Row Bearings. Single-row needle roller bearings consist of a single row of needle rollers arranged in a cage, providing high load capacity with minimal space. On the other hand, double-row needle roller bearings feature two rows of needle rollers to support heavier loads and higher radial rigidity. Both types are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial machinery for their efficiency and performance.</p></p>
