<p><p>Contact center analytics market can be classified into two types: on-premises and on-demand. On-premises analytics solutions require the installation of software and hardware within the organization's infrastructure. This gives businesses complete control over their data and customization options but requires substantial capital investment and maintenance. On the other hand, on-demand analytics, also known as cloud-based or Software-as-a-Service (SaaS), are hosted by third-party providers. They offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as businesses can access the analytics platform remotely without the need for extensive IT infrastructure or upfront costs.</p></p>
