<p><strong>The Industrial Effluent Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-site Treatment</li><li>Off-site Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial effluent treatment market can be categorized into two types: on-site treatment and off-site treatment. On-site treatment refers to the purification of industrial wastewater within the premises of the industry itself, using wastewater treatment plants and techniques. This helps to reduce the transportation cost and time required for wastewater disposal. On the other hand, off-site treatment involves transporting the wastewater to treatment plants located outside the industry for purification and disposal. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages depending on factors such as cost, infrastructure, and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
