<p><p>White spirits market is categorized into regular flash grade, high flash grade, and low flash grade. Regular flash grade white spirits have a flash point between 21- 60 degrees Celsius and are commonly used in general purpose cleaning and degreasing applications. High flash grade white spirits have a flash point above 60 degrees Celsius, making them suitable for industrial applications such as in paints, coatings, and varnishes. Low flash grade white spirits have a flash point below 21 degrees Celsius and are primarily used as a solvent in the printing industry.</p></p>
