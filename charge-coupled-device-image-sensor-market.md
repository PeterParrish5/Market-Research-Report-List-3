<p><p>Charge-Coupled Device (CCD) image sensors are used in various applications such as digital cameras, medical imaging devices, and astronomical photography. There are three main types of CCD image sensors: Frame Transfer Image Sensors, Full Frame Transfer Image Sensors, and Interline Transfer Image Sensors. Frame Transfer sensors allow for a full-frame exposure before transferring the image to a storage area, Full Frame Transfer sensors transfer the entire image at once, and Interline Transfer sensors transfer the image line by line, providing faster readout speeds.</p></p>
