<p><p>The Automotive Seat Actuation System market consists of two main types: Electric Seat Actuation System (ESAS) and Manual Seat Actuation System (MSAS). ESAS utilizes electric motors to adjust seat positions electronically, offering greater convenience and comfort for drivers and passengers. In contrast, MSAS requires manual adjustment of seat positions through levers or knobs. While ESAS systems are becoming more prevalent in modern vehicles due to their ease of use, MSAS systems still offer a cost-effective alternative for certain vehicle models.</p></p>
