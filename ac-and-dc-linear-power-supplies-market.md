<p><p>AC and DC linear power supplies are electronic devices that convert AC power into DC power with minimal noise and ripple. AC linear power supplies use a transformer to convert AC power to a lower voltage AC signal, which is then rectified and filtered to produce DC power. DC linear power supplies on the other hand, directly convert AC power into DC power using a rectifier circuit.</p><p>The AC and DC linear power supplies market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for reliable and efficient power supplies in various industries such as telecommunications, electronics, automotive, and healthcare is driving the growth of the market.
