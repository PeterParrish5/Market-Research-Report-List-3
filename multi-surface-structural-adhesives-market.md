<p><p>Multi-surface structural adhesives are classified into four main types: Epoxy, Polyurethane, Acrylic, and Methyl Methacrylate. Epoxy adhesives are known for their strong bonding capabilities and resistance to chemicals and heat. Polyurethane adhesives are flexible and have excellent impact resistance. Acrylic adhesives offer fast curing times and high strength. Methyl Methacrylate adhesives are ideal for bonding metals and plastics, with high durability and weather resistance. Each type of adhesive offers unique properties suited for different applications in the construction and manufacturing industries.</p></p>
