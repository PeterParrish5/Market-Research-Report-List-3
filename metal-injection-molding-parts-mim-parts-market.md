<p><p>Metal Injection Molding Parts (MIM Parts) are metal components manufactured using the metal injection molding process, where fine metal powders are mixed with binding agents to create a feedstock that is then injected into a mold cavity. The mixture is then debinded and sintered to produce the final metal part. MIM Parts offer high precision, complex shapes, and excellent surface finish, making them ideal for a wide range of industries including automotive, aerospace, electronics, and medical.</p><p>The Metal Injection Molding Parts (MIM Parts) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.3% during the forecast period.
