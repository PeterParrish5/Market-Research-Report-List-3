<p><p>Healthcare simulation software market consists of various types such as spreadsheets, discrete event simulators, agent-based simulators, continuous simulators, and hybrid simulators. Spreadsheets utilize data analysis and manipulation capabilities for basic simulations. Discrete event simulators model processes through analyzing events that occur in the healthcare system. Agent-based simulators focus on individual agents and their interactions within the system. Continuous simulators handle dynamic systems by considering time-dependent variables. Hybrid simulators combine features of multiple types to provide a comprehensive simulation experience in the healthcare domain.</p></p>
