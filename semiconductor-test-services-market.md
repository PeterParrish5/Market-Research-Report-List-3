<p><p>The semiconductor test services market includes various types of tests conducted during the manufacturing process. Front-end engineering testing assesses the design of the semiconductor, ensuring its functionality and performance. Wafer probing involves testing individual circuits on a wafer to identify any defects before assembly. Logic final test examines the entire semiconductor's logic functionality. System-in-package (Module) testing verifies the integration of multiple chips in a single package. Other tests may include reliability testing, burn-in testing, etc., ensuring the overall quality and reliability of the semiconductor.</p></p>
