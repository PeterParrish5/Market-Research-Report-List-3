<p><p>Food Grade Recycled PP is categorized into two main market types: Post-industrial Resin (PIR) and Post-consumption Resin (PCR). PIR refers to recycled PP sourced from industrial processes, such as manufacturing waste or excess materials. On the other hand, PCR is recycled PP that comes from post-consumption sources, like used packaging or products. Both PIR and PCR are crucial in the food industry as they enable the production of sustainable packaging and products while reducing overall environmental impact.</p></p>
