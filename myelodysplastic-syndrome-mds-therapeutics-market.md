<p><p>Myelodysplastic Syndrome (MDS) is a group of blood disorders characterized by the improper development of blood cells in the bone marrow. It leads to the formation of dysfunctional blood cells, affecting their ability to perform their respective functions. MDS can progress to a more severe condition such as acute myeloid leukemia (AML). The exact cause of MDS is still unknown, but it is believed to be linked to genetic mutations, exposure to certain chemicals, and radiation therapy.</p><p>The Myelodysplastic Syndrome (MDS) Therapeutics Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.3% during the forecast period.
