Similarly, 384 wells thermoshakers can handle plates or tubes with 384 wells, allowing for larger sample throughput. The "others" category in the market refers to thermoshakers with well capacities different from 96 and 384, catering to specific needs of researchers in fields like genomics or drug discovery.</p></p>
