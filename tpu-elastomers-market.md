<p><p>TPU elastomers market includes polyester-based type and polyether-based type. Polyester-based TPU elastomers are known for their high mechanical strength, abrasion resistance, and chemical resistance, making them suitable for applications such as automotive parts and industrial machinery components. On the other hand, polyether-based TPU elastomers offer excellent flexibility, low-temperature resistance, and hydrolytic stability, making them ideal for applications in footwear, sporting goods, and medical devices. Both types cater to unique market needs and offer a wide range of benefits for various industries.</p></p>
