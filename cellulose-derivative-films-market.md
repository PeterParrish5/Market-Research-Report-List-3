<p><p>Cellulose derivative films can be categorized into two main types: coated films and uncoated films. Coated films are treated with a layer of coating material to enhance properties such as moisture resistance, barrier protection, and printability. Uncoated films, on the other hand, do not have any additional coating and are typically used for applications where a natural, transparent look is desired. Both types of films offer unique advantages and are widely used in various industries such as packaging, pharmaceuticals, and food.</p></p>
