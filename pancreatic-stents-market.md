These conditions often lead to the development of blockages in the pancreatic duct, necessitating the use of stents for treatment.</p><p>Another factor driving market growth is the technological advancements in the design of pancreatic stents. <p><p>Pancreatic stents are medical devices used to treat conditions affecting the pancreas. There are three main types of pancreatic stents: straight, wedge, and curved. Straight pancreatic stents are designed to maintain the patency of a straight pancreatic duct. Wedge pancreatic stents are used in cases where there is a tumor obstructing the main pancreatic duct. Curved pancreatic stents are specifically shaped to accommodate the anatomy of the pancreatic duct. Each type of stent serves a specific purpose in managing various pancreatic conditions.</p></p>
