<p><p>Duct air quality sensors are important devices that help monitor and maintain healthy indoor air quality. There are two main types of duct air quality sensors: capacitive sensors and resistance sensors. Capacitive sensors use changes in electrical capacitance to detect air quality, while resistance sensors measure changes in electrical resistance. Both types of sensors are crucial in detecting pollutants such as particulate matter, VOCs, and humidity levels in duct systems, ensuring optimal air quality for building occupants.</p></p>
