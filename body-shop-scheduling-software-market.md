<p><p>Body shop scheduling software is available in two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted and accessed via the internet, allowing users to access it from anywhere using any device. It offers flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates but requires an internet connection. On-premises software is installed and hosted locally on the premises of the body shop. It offers enhanced control, data security, and offline access but requires dedicated server infrastructure and regular maintenance. Body shops can choose the market type that best suits their needs and preferences.</p></p>
