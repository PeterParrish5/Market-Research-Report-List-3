<p><p>Commercial Heating Boilers Market consists of two main types: Non-condensing Boilers and Condensing Boilers. Non-condensing Boilers are traditional boilers that release exhaust gases through a chimney, while Condensing Boilers are more energy-efficient as they utilize heat from exhaust gases to heat water, resulting in lower energy consumption and emissions. The Condensing Boilers market is growing rapidly due to increasing environmental concerns and government regulations promoting energy-efficient heating systems, while Non-condensing Boilers are still widely used in commercial settings.</p></p>
