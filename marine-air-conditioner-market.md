<p><ul><li>Integral Type Air Conditioner</li><li>Split Type Air Conditioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine air conditioner market includes two main types: integral type air conditioner and split type air conditioner. Integral type air conditioners are compact units with all components housed in a single casing, making them easy to install and maintain. On the other hand, split type air conditioners have separate indoor and outdoor units connected by refrigerant lines, offering more flexibility in installation and better cooling efficiency. Both types are commonly used in marine applications to ensure comfortable temperature control on boats and ships.</p></p>
