<p><p>Flexible epoxy adhesive market can be segmented into one-component adhesive and two-component adhesive. One-component adhesive is a pre-mixed adhesive that is ready to use and requires no additional preparation. Two-component adhesive, on the other hand, consists of two separate components that need to be mixed together before application. One-component adhesive is convenient and easy to use, while two-component adhesive offers better bonding strength and versatility for more complex projects. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in the market for flexible epoxy adhesives.</p></p>
