<p><p>A bone fixation plate is a medical device used in orthopedic surgeries to stabilize fractured bones during the healing process. These plates are typically made of stainless steel or titanium and are available in different shapes and sizes for specific applications. They are attached to the bone using screws and serve as an internal splint to maintain the alignment of fractured bone segments.</p><p>The bone fixation plate market has been witnessing significant growth due to the rising prevalence of orthopedic conditions such as fractures and the increasing number of surgeries. The growing geriatric population, who are susceptible to bone fractures, is also contributing to market growth. Technological advancements in bone fixation plate designs and materials are further propelling the market.</p><p>One of the key trends in the bone fixation plate market is the adoption of biodegradable plates. These plates are manufactured from materials that are gradually absorbed by the body, eliminating the need for a second surgery to remove them. The use of biodegradable plates reduces the risk of infection and improves patient comfort. Furthermore, the development of customizable and patient-specific fixation plates is gaining traction in the market, as these plates offer enhanced surgical outcomes and reduce the risk of complications.</p><p>The market for bone fixation plates is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period.
