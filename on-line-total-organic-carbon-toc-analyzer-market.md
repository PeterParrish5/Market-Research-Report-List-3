<p><p>The On-line Total Organic Carbon (TOC) Analyzer market includes three main types: Ultraviolet Oxidation, UV Persulfate Oxidation, and High-temperature Combustion. Ultraviolet Oxidation uses UV light to break down organic compounds, while UV Persulfate Oxidation combines UV light with persulfate to oxidize organics. High-temperature Combustion completely burns organic material to measure TOC levels. Each type offers unique advantages and applications, catering to different industries and environmental monitoring needs. These analyzers provide real-time and accurate data on organic carbon content in water and wastewater.</p></p>
