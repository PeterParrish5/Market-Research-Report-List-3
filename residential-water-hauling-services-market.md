<p><p>Residential water hauling services offer two main market types: water truck services and vacuum truck services. Water truck services involve transporting large volumes of water to residential areas using specialized trucks equipped with water tanks. These services are commonly used for filling swimming pools, irrigating lawns, or supplying water during emergencies. On the other hand, vacuum truck services are focused on removing and disposing of liquid waste, such as septic waste or grease trap waste, from residential properties. These trucks use vacuum systems to efficiently suck up and transport the waste to appropriate disposal facilities.</p></p>
