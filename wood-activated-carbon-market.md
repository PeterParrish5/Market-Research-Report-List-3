<p><ul><li>Powdered</li><li>Granular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wood activated carbon is available in two main market types: powdered and granular. Powdered activated carbon consists of very fine particles that provide high surface area for adsorption, making it ideal for applications where a fast reaction time is needed, such as in water and air purification systems. Granular activated carbon, on the other hand, consists of larger particles that offer longer contact time and are commonly used in gas phase applications, such as gas purification and solvent recovery. Both types provide efficient adsorption properties for various industries.</p></p>
