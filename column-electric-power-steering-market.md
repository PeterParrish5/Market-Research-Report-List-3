<p><p>Column-Electric Power Steering is divided into two main types: Brushed Motor and Brushless Motor market. In Brushed Motor systems, a motor with brushes and a commutator is used to provide the necessary force for steering assistance. On the other hand, Brushless Motor systems utilize electronic commutation instead of brushes, offering improved efficiency and reliability. Both types have their own advantages and are commonly used in modern vehicles to provide seamless and responsive steering assistance to drivers.</p></p>
