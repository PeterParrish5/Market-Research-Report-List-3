<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Auto Lensometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Visual Type Lensmeter</li><li>Projection Type Lensmeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Lensometer market consists of two types: Visual Type Lensmeter and Projection Type Lensmeter. Visual Type Lensmeter is a handheld device used to measure the optical properties of lenses by observing the images they create. It features a viewing window, a prism, and a measurement scale. In contrast, Projection Type Lensmeter is stationary equipment that measures lens power by projecting light through the lens and analyzing the refraction pattern. This type offers higher accuracy and efficiency compared to the visual type. Both types are widely used in optometry and eyewear production industries.</p></p>
