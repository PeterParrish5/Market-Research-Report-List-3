<p><p>PVC Insulated Power Cable Market offers three main types: Single Strand, Double Strands, and Multiple Strands. Single Strand cables consist of a single conductor, suitable for simple applications where flexibility is not a concern. Double Strand cables have two conductors twisted together, providing increased flexibility and durability. Multiple Strand cables consist of multiple conductors braided together, offering even greater flexibility and resilience. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements in the power cable market.</p></p>
