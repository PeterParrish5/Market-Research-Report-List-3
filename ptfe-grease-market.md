<p><strong>The PTFE Grease Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dispersion Method</li><li>Suspension Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PTFE grease is classified into two types based on the method of dispersion - Dispersion Method and Suspension Method. In the Dispersion Method, PTFE particles are dispersed in a liquid lubricating medium to form a stable emulsion. On the other hand, in the Suspension Method, PTFE particles are suspended in a liquid to form a stable colloidal suspension. Both methods offer effective lubrication properties and are widely used in various industrial applications for reducing friction, wear, and improving overall performance.</p></p>
