<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Diagnostic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave Ultrasound Equipment</li><li>Pulsed Wave Ultrasound Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic diagnostic equipment market is categorized into two main types: Continuous wave ultrasound equipment and pulsed wave ultrasound equipment. Continuous wave ultrasound equipment emits a continuous stream of ultrasound waves, which are used for Doppler imaging and continuous waveform analysis. Pulsed wave ultrasound equipment, on the other hand, emits short bursts of ultrasound waves at regular intervals, allowing for more detailed imaging and depth penetration. Both types of equipment are essential in medical diagnostics and provide healthcare professionals with valuable information for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
