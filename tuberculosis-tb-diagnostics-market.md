<p><p>The Tuberculosis (TB) Diagnostics market consists of several types of diagnostic methods. Culture-Based Diagnostics involve growing bacteria from a patient's sputum sample in a lab to detect TB. Sputum Smear Microscopy examines sputum under a microscope to identify TB bacteria. Rapid Molecular Diagnostics utilize molecular methods to quickly identify TB bacteria in a patient's sample. Other types of diagnostics include serological tests, nucleic acid amplification tests, chest X-rays, and molecular line probe assays. These methods help in the early detection and prompt treatment of TB.</p></p>
