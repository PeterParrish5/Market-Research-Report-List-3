<p><p>The auto-injector market consists of two main types: disposable and reusable auto-injectors. Disposable auto-injectors are designed for single-use only and typically come pre-filled with medication. After use, they are discarded. On the other hand, reusable auto-injectors are designed to be used multiple times. They usually require a new cartridge or refill to be loaded after each use. Both types of auto-injectors are commonly used to administer medication easily and safely, particularly for emergency situations or for people with chronic conditions.</p></p>
