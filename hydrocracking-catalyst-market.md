<p><p>Hydrocracking Catalyst market can be categorized into two types: Transition Metal Catalyst and Precious Metal Catalyst. Transition metal catalysts are typically less expensive and commonly used in hydrocracking processes for converting heavy oil into lighter and more valuable products. On the other hand, precious metal catalysts are more efficient and selective, making them preferred for high-performance hydrocracking applications where yield quality is critical. Both types play a crucial role in the refining industry, catering to different needs based on cost and performance requirements.</p></p>
