<p><p>The automotive brake lining market comprises two main types: Semimetal Type and NAO Type. Semimetal brake linings are made of a mix of non-asbestos organic fibers and metal fibers, providing strong stopping power and durability. NAO (Non-Asbestos Organic) brake linings are made of organic fibers, fillers, and resins, offering a quieter and cleaner braking experience. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences, with each offering a unique set of benefits in terms of performance and environmental impact.</p></p>
