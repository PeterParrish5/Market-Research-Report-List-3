<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Transdermal Patch Drug Delivery System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Way</li><li>Intercellular Lipid Structure Pathway</li><li>Trans-cellular Pathway</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transdermal Patch Drug Delivery System market consists of three types: Shunt Way, Intercellular Lipid Structure Pathway, and Trans-cellular Pathway. The Shunt Way type involves the drug penetrating the hair follicles and sweat glands to enter the bloodstream. The Intercellular Lipid Structure Pathway entails drugs passing through the tiny spaces between skin cells. Lastly, the Trans-cellular Pathway involves drugs penetrating both skin cells and the lipid structure and entering the bloodstream. These different pathways offer various options for drug delivery through the skin in the Transdermal Patch Drug Delivery System market.</p></p>
