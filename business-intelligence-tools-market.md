<p><p>Business Intelligence (BI) tools encompass two main market types: Cloud Based and On-Premise. Cloud-based BI tools are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet. They offer scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness, as users pay subscriptions rather than investing in infrastructure. On the other hand, On-Premise BI tools are installed on local servers and managed internally. They provide full data control, but require higher upfront costs and maintenance. Both market types aim to enable businesses to gather, analyze, and visualize data for informed decision-making.</p></p>
