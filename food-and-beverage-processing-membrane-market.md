<p><p>Food and Beverage Processing Membrane Market consists of Polymeric Membranes and Ceramic Membranes. Polymeric membranes are made of synthetic materials like polysulfone and polyethersulfone, offering cost-effective filtration solutions for food and beverage processing. Ceramic membranes, on the other hand, are made of inorganic materials like alumina and zirconia, providing superior durability and resistance to harsh chemicals. Both types of membranes play a crucial role in the food and beverage industry by helping to separate and purify liquids for various applications.</p></p>
