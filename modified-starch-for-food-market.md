<p><p>Modified starch for food market types include compound modified starch, oxidized starch, cationic starch, acetate starch, phosphate starch, pregelatinized starch, and other specialty modified starches. Compound modified starch is a combination of different modifications for specific functionalities. Oxidized starch is chemically altered for improved stability. Cationic starch has positive charges for enhanced binding properties. Acetate starch is acetylated for better film-forming abilities. Phosphate starch is phosphorylated for better thickening. Pregelatinized starch is pre-cooked for easy dissolvability. Other specialty modified starches cater to unique food applications.</p></p>
