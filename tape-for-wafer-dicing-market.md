<p><strong>The Tape for Wafer Dicing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV</li><li>Non-UV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tape for wafer dicing is used in the semiconductor industry for separating individual chips from a silicon wafer during the manufacturing process. There are two main types of tape: UV and Non-UV. UV tapes are used for wafer dicing with UV laser cutting machines, offering high precision and clean cuts. Non-UV tapes are suitable for conventional dicing processes using mechanical saws. Each type of tape serves different requirements in terms of equipment compatibility and precision in wafer separation.</p></p>
