<p><p>Surgical navigation equipment is categorized into two main types: optical surgical navigation equipment and electromagnetic surgical navigation equipment. Optical surgical navigation equipment uses cameras and infrared light to track the position of surgical instruments in real time. This enables precise tracking and guidance during surgical procedures. Meanwhile, electromagnetic surgical navigation equipment utilizes electromagnetic fields to track the position of instruments and provide accurate guidance. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in enhancing surgical precision and reducing the risks involved in complex procedures.</p></p>
