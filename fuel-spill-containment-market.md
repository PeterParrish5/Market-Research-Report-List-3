<p><p>Fuel spill containment market can be categorized into two main types: Remote-Fill Type and Direct-Fill Type. Remote-Fill Type containment systems are designed to prevent spills during the refilling process by allowing fuel to be filled from a distance, reducing the risk of spillage. On the other hand, Direct-Fill Type systems are designed for direct refilling of fuel without the need for additional equipment, providing a quick and efficient way to prevent spills. Both types aim to minimize environmental impact and ensure safe refueling practices.</p></p>
