<p><strong>The Textile Leveling Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fibrophilic Leveling Agents</li><li>Dye-Philic Leveling Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Textile leveling agents are used to ensure even dyeing of fabrics by promoting the uniform penetration of dyes. Fibrophilic leveling agents are designed to adhere better to the fibers of the fabric, allowing for more efficient dye absorption. On the other hand, dye-philic leveling agents are formulated to have a stronger affinity for the dye molecules, facilitating their dispersion and absorption on the fabric surface. Both types of leveling agents play a crucial role in achieving consistent and high-quality dyeing results in the textile industry.</p></p>
