<p><p>Dunnage Bags are used in various industries to secure and stabilize cargo during transportation. There are three main types of Dunnage Bags available in the market: Poly-woven, Kraft Paper, and Vinyl. Poly-woven Dunnage Bags are made from durable, tear-resistant woven polypropylene material, offering high strength and reliability. Kraft Paper Dunnage Bags are composed of multiple layers of kraft paper with an inner plastic liner, providing a cost-effective solution while still maintaining good strength. Vinyl Dunnage Bags are constructed using heavy-duty vinyl material, ensuring exceptional durability and resistance to punctures. These market options cater to different requirements based on strength, cost, and specific cargo needs.</p></p>
