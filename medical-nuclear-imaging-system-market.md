<p><p>The Medical Nuclear Imaging System market consists of three main types: SPECT Systems, Hybrid PET Systems, and Planar Scintigraphy market. SPECT Systems utilize a gamma camera and a radiopharmaceutical to produce detailed images of internal organs and their functionality. Hybrid PET Systems combine positron emission tomography (PET) and computed tomography (CT) technologies to provide both anatomical and functional information of the body. Planar Scintigraphy involves the use of a gamma camera to capture two-dimensional images showing the distribution of a radiopharmaceutical in the body.</p></p>
