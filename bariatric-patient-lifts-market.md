<p><p>Bariatric patient lifts are specialized medical devices used for transferring and lifting patients of larger sizes in healthcare settings. There are three main types: hydraulic, electric, and battery powered. Hydraulic bariatric lifts use hydraulic technology to lift and lower patients, providing a cost-effective option. Electric bariatric lifts operate using an electric motor, allowing for smoother and easier transfers. Battery powered bariatric lifts are similar to electric ones but are equipped with rechargeable batteries, providing more flexibility and mobility in patient transfers. These lifts are vital in ensuring the safety and proper care of bariatric patients in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
