<p><p>An ultrasonic scalpel, also known as a harmonic scalpel, is a surgical instrument that uses ultrasonic vibrations to cut and coagulate tissues during surgical procedures. It works by converting electrical energy into mechanical vibrations, which are transmitted to the blade, causing it to vibrate at a high frequency. This vibration creates heat, which simultaneously cuts and cauterizes the tissue, leading to less bleeding, reduced damage to surrounding tissues, and faster healing.</p><p>The ultrasonic scalpel market is expected to experience significant growth over the forecast period. Factors such as the increasing number of surgical procedures, advancements in technology, and the rising demand for minimally invasive surgeries are driving the market growth.
