<p><p>The ETFE Membrane Market can be categorized into three types based on thickness - Below 200 µm, 200-300 µm, and Above 300 µm. Membranes in the 200-300 µm range provide a balance between flexibility and durability, suitable for a wide range of applications. Membranes above 300 µm are thicker and offer increased strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty or long-term use.</p></p>
