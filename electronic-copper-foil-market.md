<p><p>Electronic Copper Foil market consists of two main types: Rolled Copper Foil and Electrolytic Copper Foil. Rolled Copper Foil is made by pressing copper ingots at high temperatures, resulting in a thin and flexible foil that is commonly used in printed circuit boards. On the other hand, Electrolytic Copper Foil is produced through an electrolysis process, yielding a high purity copper foil that is suitable for high-frequency applications in electronics. Both types play a crucial role in the electronic industry.</p></p>
