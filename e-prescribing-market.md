<p><p>E-prescribing market includes stand-alone systems and integrated systems. Stand-alone systems are software applications used specifically for electronic prescribing purposes. They are independent platforms that can be used separately or in conjunction with other healthcare systems. On the other hand, integrated systems are part of electronic health record (EHR) systems, combining e-prescribing functionality with other healthcare management features. These integrated systems streamline the prescribing process by eliminating the need for separate software and allowing healthcare providers to access patient information easily while prescribing medication.</p></p>
