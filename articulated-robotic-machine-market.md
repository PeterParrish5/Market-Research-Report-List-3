<p><p>Articulated robotic machines are classified based on the number of axes they possess for movement. Less than 4-axis machines have limited movement capabilities, while 4-axis machines can move along four different axes. 5-axis machines offer more flexibility and precision in their movements, while 6-axis machines are capable of complex maneuvers and intricate tasks. Machines with more than 6 axes provide the highest level of flexibility and versatility, making them ideal for advanced and specialized applications.</p></p>
