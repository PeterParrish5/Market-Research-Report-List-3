<p><strong>The Corneal Topographers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pacido-based Systems</li><li>Evaluation-based Systems</li><li>Interferometric Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corneal topographers are used in eye care to measure and map the curvature of the cornea. There are three types of corneal topographers in the market. Pacido-based systems use a series of concentric rings to project onto the cornea and capture images that are analyzed to determine the shape of the cornea. Evaluation-based systems utilize multiple light sources and sensors to derive corneal measurements. Interferometric systems use interference patterns created by light waves to measure corneal shape. Each system offers unique advantages and can provide valuable information for diagnosing and treating various eye conditions.</p></p>
