<p><ul><li>IGBT Modules</li><li>SiC Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Power Modules refer to electronic components used in vehicle power systems to control and manage power flows. Two common types of Automotive Power Modules are IGBT Modules and SiC Modules. IGBT Modules are insulated-gate bipolar transistors that offer high efficiency and fast switching speeds, making them ideal for electric vehicle applications. SiC Modules, on the other hand, use silicon carbide technology to provide lower power losses and higher temperature tolerance, making them suitable for high-performance and high-power automotive applications.</p></p>
