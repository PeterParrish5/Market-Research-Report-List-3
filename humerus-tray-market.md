<p><p>The humerus tray market consists of three types: standard, lateralized, and extra lateralized. </p><p>Standard trays are designed to accommodate the average anatomy of patients and provide a stable fit. Lateralized trays are modified to increase offset and improve stability for patients with specific biomechanical needs. This design allows for better joint function and reduces the risk of dislocation. Extra lateralized trays are further modified to provide even more offset and stability, making them suitable for patients with severe bone loss or complex anatomical deformities. These trays aim to improve patient outcomes by providing a customized fit and enhancing overall joint stability.</p></p>
