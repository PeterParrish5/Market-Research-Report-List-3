<p><p>Self-checkout counters come in a variety of types, including fluorescence detection and magnetic detection markets. In fluorescence detection, the counter uses a light source to illuminate items with fluorescent properties, making them easier to scan and detect. Magnetic detection, on the other hand, uses magnetic sensors to identify products by their unique magnetic signatures. Both technologies are integrated into self-checkout counters to provide fast and accurate item identification, enhancing the overall shopping experience for customers.</p></p>
