<p><ul><li>Organic HPPs</li><li>Inorganic HPPs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-performance pigments (HPPs) can be categorized into two main market types: organic HPPs and inorganic HPPs. Organic HPPs are derived from carbon-based compounds and are known for their excellent color strength, heat stability, and lightfastness. They are widely used in industries like automotive coatings and printing inks. On the other hand, inorganic HPPs are made from minerals and provide exceptional durability, chemical resistance, and weather-resistance properties. Due to these qualities, they are commonly used in applications such as architectural coatings, plastics, and ceramics.</p></p>
