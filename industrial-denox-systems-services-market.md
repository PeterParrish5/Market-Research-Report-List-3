<p><p>Industrial DeNOx systems and services are used to control nitrogen oxide (NOx) emissions from industrial sources, such as power plants and manufacturing facilities. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) and Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction (SNCR) are two commonly used types of DeNOx systems. SCR systems use a catalyst to convert NOx gases into nitrogen and water vapor, while SNCR systems inject ammonia or urea into the combustion process to chemically reduce NOx emissions. Both systems help industries meet regulatory requirements, reduce air pollution, and improve overall environmental performance.</p></p>
