Carbon fiber nonwoven mats are created by layering carbon fibers in a random orientation to form a lightweight and strong material. Chopped carbon fiber consists of short strands of carbon fiber that can be easily mixed into various composites. Milled carbon fiber involves grinding carbon fiber into a fine powder for use in various applications such as 3D printing and reinforcement materials.</p></p>
