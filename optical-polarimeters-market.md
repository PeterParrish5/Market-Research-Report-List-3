<p><p>Optical polarimeters are instruments used to measure the optical rotation of polarized light passing through a substance. They are available in two main types - single wavelength and double wavelength polarimeters. Single wavelength polarimeters use one specific wavelength of light to determine the optical rotation of a sample, while double wavelength polarimeters use two different wavelengths for more precise measurements. Both types of polarimeters are used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, and research laboratories for analyzing the optical properties of substances.</p></p>
