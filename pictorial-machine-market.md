<p><p>Pictorial Machine is a term used to describe machines used in printing images or designs on various surfaces. Thermal Foaming Pictorial Machines utilize heat to transfer designs onto objects, while Piezo Pictorial Machines use a piezoelectric crystal to create pressure and produce high-quality prints. Both these market types cater to different needs and preferences in the printing industry. Thermal Foaming machines are popular for their efficient heating capabilities, while Piezo machines are known for their precise and detailed prints, making them suitable for applications requiring finer image quality.</p></p>
