<p><p>The emergency and trauma stretchers market can be categorized into two main types: hydraulic stretchers and manual stretchers. Hydraulic stretchers are designed to be adjustable in height and can be easily raised or lowered using a hydraulic mechanism. On the other hand, manual stretchers require physical force to adjust the height and are operated manually by healthcare professionals. Both types of stretchers serve the purpose of transferring patients in emergency and trauma situations, but they differ in terms of the mechanism used for height adjustment.</p></p>
