<p><p>Industrial wireless power transmission for short-range refers to the transfer of electrical energy without the need for physical connections. Two market types in this field are electromagnetic induction technology and magnetic field coupling technology. Electro-magnetic induction technology involves using magnetic fields generated by a transmitter coil to induce current in a receiver coil, while magnetic field coupling technology utilizes a magnetic field generated by a transmitter antenna to transfer power to a receiver antenna. Both technologies provide efficient and convenient power transfer solutions for industrial applications within a limited range.</p></p>
