<p><p>The Melanoma Scanner market offers various types of scanners for detecting different subtypes of melanoma. Superficial Spreading Melanoma scanners are designed to identify the most common type that grows horizontally on the skin. Nodular Melanoma scanners focus on detecting the aggressive type that develops as a raised bump. Lentigo Maligna scanners target the slowest growing subtype that appears on sun-exposed areas of the skin. Acral Lentiginous Melanoma scanners are specifically developed to identify the rare type that affects the palms, soles, or under the nails. These scanners help in early diagnosis and effective treatment of melanoma.</p></p>
