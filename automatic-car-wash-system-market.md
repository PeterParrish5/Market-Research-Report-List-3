<p><p>The Gantry Car Wash System is a type of automatic car wash system where the vehicle remains stationary while the equipment moves around it. This system is popular for its speed and efficiency in cleaning cars. On the other hand, the Conveyor Tunnel System involves the vehicle being driven onto a conveyor belt that moves it through various stages of the washing process. This system is favored for its ability to handle a high volume of cars efficiently.</p></p>
