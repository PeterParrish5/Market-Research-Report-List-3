<p><p>Disposable colostomy bags come in two main types: one-piece pouches and two-piece pouches. One-piece pouches are designed with the bag and skin barrier attached together, making them easier to apply and dispose of. On the other hand, two-piece pouches consist of a separate skin barrier and bag, allowing for more customization and flexibility in changing the bag without replacing the entire appliance. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of colostomy patients in the market.</p></p>
