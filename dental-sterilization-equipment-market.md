<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental sterilization equipment market consists of two types: semi-automatic and automatic. Semi-automatic equipment requires manual intervention for the sterilization process, where operators load and unload instruments. This type is usually preferred by smaller dental practices. On the other hand, automatic equipment offers fully automated sterilization, reducing the need for manual intervention. This type is suitable for larger dental clinics or hospitals with higher instrument volumes. Both types ensure the utmost level of sterilization, but the choice depends on the size and specific needs of the dental facility.</p></p>
