<p><p>A transdermal patch is a type of drug delivery system that continuously releases medication through the skin into the bloodstream. The increasing prevalence of chronic diseases such as cardiovascular diseases, pain management, and hormonal disorders, along with the growing geriatric population, are driving the demand for transdermal patches. These patches are now being used for a wide variety of applications, including nicotine replacement therapy, contraception, motion sickness, and neurological disorders.
