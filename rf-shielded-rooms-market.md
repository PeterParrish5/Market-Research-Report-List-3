<p><p>RF shielded rooms are designed to block electromagnetic interference and maintain privacy within the enclosed space. There are three types of markets for RF shielded rooms based on the mechanism for opening and closing the room door. Manual market refers to rooms with manually operated doors, usually requiring physical strength to open or close. Pneumatic market utilizes air pressure to automatically control the opening and closing of the door. Electric market involves electronically operated doors, enabling convenient and effortless access to the shielded rooms. These different types cater to diverse user preferences and ease of handling.</p></p>
