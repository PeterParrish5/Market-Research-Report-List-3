<p><p>The ophthalmic excimer laser curer market consists of various types of lasers used for treating eye conditions. Diode lasers emit low-energy beams for photocoagulation and retinal surgery. Femtosecond lasers operate at ultrafast speeds, making them effective for cataract and refractive surgeries. Excimer lasers are commonly used for corneal reshaping procedures, such as LASIK. Nd:YAG lasers are used for posterior capsulotomy and iridotomy. Argon lasers emit blue-green light and are employed for retinal photocoagulation. SLT lasers are used for selective laser trabeculoplasty to lower intraocular pressure and manage glaucoma.</p></p>
