<p><p>Gluten intolerance treatment market is comprised of various types, including antihistamines and epinephrine. Antihistamines are medications that help reduce or block the effects of histamine, a chemical released by the immune system during an allergic reaction. These can alleviate symptoms like itching, sneezing, and runny nose caused by gluten intolerance. Epinephrine, on the other hand, is a hormone that can be injected in emergency situations to treat severe allergic reactions by constricting blood vessels and opening airways. Both antihistamines and epinephrine play important roles in managing gluten intolerance symptoms.</p></p>
