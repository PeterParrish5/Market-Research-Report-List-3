<p><strong>The Connector Protection Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connector protection devices are divided into two main types in the market: Type I and Type II. Type I devices are designed to protect against overvoltage events caused by lightning strikes or other transient surges. They are typically installed at the service entrance of a building to safeguard against external threats. Type II devices, on the other hand, are used to protect against internal transient surges generated within a building's electrical system. These devices are installed at branch circuits or individual equipment to prevent damage to sensitive electronics.</p></p>
