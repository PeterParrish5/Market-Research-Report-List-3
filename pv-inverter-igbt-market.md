<p><p>The PV inverter IGBT market can be classified into two main types: IGBT modules and IGBT discretes. IGBT modules are complete units that contain multiple IGBT chips and other components within a single package, providing higher power density and efficiency. On the other hand, IGBT discretes are individual components that offer more flexibility and customization options for specific applications. Both types play a crucial role in converting DC power from solar panels into usable AC power for residential, commercial, and utility-scale solar PV systems.</p></p>
