<p><h1>Pipe Jacking Service Market: A Comprehensive Report of its Market Share & Growth Trends 2024 - 2031</h1></p><p><strong>Pipe Jacking Service Market Analysis and Latest Trends</strong></p>
<p><p>Pipe jacking service is a construction method used to install underground pipelines without the need for open trench excavation. It involves pushing pipes through the ground using hydraulic jacks, which reduces disruption to the surface area and minimizes environmental impact. This method is commonly used for installing sewer, water, and gas pipelines in urban areas where traditional excavation methods are not feasible.</p><p>The pipe jacking service market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for underground infrastructure in both developed and developing countries. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period, driven by factors such as urbanization, population growth, and the need to replace aging pipeline networks. Additionally, advancements in technology and equipment used for pipe jacking are also fueling market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the pipe jacking service market include the adoption of microtunneling technology, which allows for the installation of larger diameter pipes over longer distances, as well as the use of environmentally friendly materials for pipeline construction. Overall, the pipe jacking service market is poised for continued expansion in the coming years as infrastructure projects continue to drive demand for efficient and sustainable underground pipeline installation methods.</p></p>
