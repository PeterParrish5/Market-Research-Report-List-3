<p><strong>The Automotive Magnet Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Enamelled Wire</li><li>Covered Conductor Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Magnet Wire Market consists of two main types: Enamelled Wire and Covered Conductor Wire. Enamelled Wire is coated with a thin layer of insulation to prevent the wire from short-circuiting when wound into coils. It is commonly used in electric motors and transformers in automobiles. Covered Conductor Wire, on the other hand, has an additional layer of insulation, such as nylon or polyester, for added protection against abrasion and environmental factors. This type of wire is often used in automotive wiring harnesses and other electrical components.</p></p>
