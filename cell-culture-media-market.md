<p><p>Cell culture media market types include classical media & salts, serum-free media, stem cell media, and other markets. Classical media & salts contain various nutrients and salts required for cell growth. Serum-free media do not contain any animal-derived components like serum and can be used for specific culture requirements. Stem cell media is designed to support the growth and differentiation of stem cells. Other markets refer to specialized media used for particular cell culture applications, such as insect cell culture or plant cell culture. These different types of media play essential roles in promoting cell growth and maintaining cell viability for various research and industrial purposes.</p></p>
