Moreover, cloud-based coding and billing solutions are gaining traction due to their scalability, cost-effectiveness, and ability to streamline workflows.</p><p>The COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated market growth, as healthcare facilities focus on digitization and automation to manage increased patient volumes and ensure efficient revenue cycle management.
