<p><strong>The Anesthesia Disposables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LMAs</li><li>Breathing Circuits</li><li>ETTs</li><li>Resuscitators</li><li>Anesthesia Face Masks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anesthesia Disposables market is composed of various types of medical devices used during anesthesia procedures. LMAs (Laryngeal Mask Airways) are inserted into the patient's throat to maintain an open airway. Breathing circuits deliver gases to and remove waste from the patient's lungs. ETTs (Endotracheal Tubes) are placed into the patient's trachea to secure the airway. Resuscitators are used to manually assist a patient's breathing. Anesthesia Face Masks are placed over the patient's nose and mouth to deliver anesthesia and maintain a clear airway.</p></p>
