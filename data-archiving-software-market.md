<p><p>Data archiving software market types can be classified into cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based archiving software is delivered as a service over the internet, allowing organizations to store and access their data remotely on cloud servers. On the other hand, on-premises archiving software is installed and maintained locally within an organization's infrastructure, giving them direct control over their data storage and security. Both types offer different advantages and considerations, with cloud-based solutions offering scalability and flexibility, while on-premises solutions provide enhanced control and data sovereignty.</p></p>
