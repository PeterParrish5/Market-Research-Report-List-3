<p><p>Filtration fabrics can be categorized into different market types based on their weave structure. Monolayer weave fabrics consist of a single layer of fibers woven together, offering basic filtration capabilities. Double weave fabrics have two layers of fibers woven together, providing increased filtration efficiency. Three-layer weave fabrics feature three layers of fibers woven together, offering superior filtration performance. Other market types include specialty fabrics designed for specific filtration needs, such as high temperature resistance or chemical compatibility. Each type of fabric serves a specific purpose in the filtration industry.</p></p>
