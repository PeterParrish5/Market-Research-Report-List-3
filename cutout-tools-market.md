<p><p>Cutout tools are available in two main types - corded and cordless. Corded cutout tools are powered by electricity through a cord, providing consistent power for prolonged use. They are ideal for tasks that require continuous power, such as longer projects or professional use. On the other hand, cordless cutout tools run on rechargeable batteries, offering greater portability and freedom of movement. They are suitable for smaller projects or tasks in locations without easy access to power outlets. Both types have their advantages and are suitable for different applications.</p></p>
