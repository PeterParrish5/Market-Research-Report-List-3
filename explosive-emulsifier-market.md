<p><ul><li>Polyisobutenyl Succinic Anhydride (PIBSA) Based</li><li>Span 80</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Explosive emulsifier market types include two common categories: Polyisobutenyl Succinic Anhydride (PIBSA) Based and Span 80. PIBSA-based emulsifiers are derived from polyisobutylene and succinic anhydride, known for their ability to stabilize water-in-oil emulsions. On the other hand, Span 80 is a sorbitan monooleate surfactant that is widely used in the explosives industry for its emulsifying properties. Both types play crucial roles in the manufacturing of explosives by creating stable emulsions that ensure proper mixing and detonation.</p></p>
