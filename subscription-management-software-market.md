<p><strong>The Subscription Management Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Premise</li><li>Cloud-Based</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Subscription management software is available in three different market types: on-premise, cloud-based, and hybrid.</p><p>On-premise subscription management software is installed and operated on the customer's own servers and infrastructure, allowing them to have complete control and customization of the software.</p><p>Cloud-based subscription management software is hosted and accessed via the internet, eliminating the need for hardware or software installation. It offers flexibility, scalability, and easy access from anywhere, with data stored on secure servers.</p><p>Hybrid subscription management software combines the benefits of both on-premise and cloud-based solutions, allowing organizations to store sensitive data on-premise while leveraging the scalability and accessibility of the cloud for other functions.</p></p>
