<p><p>Walking rotary cultivators are available in three main types - corded electric, fuel powered, and battery powered. Corded electric models require a power source and are best suited for smaller gardens. Fuel powered cultivators provide more mobility and are ideal for larger areas or areas without easy access to electricity. Battery powered models offer a compromise between the two, providing freedom of movement without the hassle of cords or the need for fuel.
