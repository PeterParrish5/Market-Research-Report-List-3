<p><strong>The Copper Foil for Printed Circuit Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper foil for printed circuit boards can be classified into two main types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced by rolling a piece of copper ingot into a thin sheet, making it ideal for flexible circuits. On the other hand, electrolytic copper foil is created through an electroplating process, resulting in a smoother and more uniform surface, making it suitable for rigid circuit boards. Each type caters to different PCB applications and requirements.</p></p>
