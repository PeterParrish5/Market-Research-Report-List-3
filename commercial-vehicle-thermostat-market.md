<p><ul><li>Insert Thermostat</li><li>Housing Thermostat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial vehicle thermostats are essential components that regulate the engine temperature, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency. Thermostats can be categorized into two main types: Insert Thermostats and Housing Thermostats. Insert thermostats are installed directly into the engine block, while housing thermostats are housed within a separate housing unit connected to the engine. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the engine temperature within the desired range, preventing overheating or excessive cooling that can lead to engine damage.</p></p>
