<p><p>Industrial grade zirconium oxide is classified into two main types: fused zirconium oxide and chemical zirconium oxide. Fused zirconium oxide is produced by melting zircon sand and is known for its high density and hardness, making it suitable for applications requiring durability such as grinding wheels and refractory bricks. On the other hand, chemical zirconium oxide is produced through chemical processes and is used in industries like ceramics, electronics, and chemicals due to its high purity and fine particle size.</p></p>
