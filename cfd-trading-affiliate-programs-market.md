<p><p>CFD Trading Affiliate Programs offer different market types for affiliates to earn commission. CPA (Cost per Acquisition) pays affiliates a commission for every customer they refer who makes a deposit and starts trading. CPL (Cost per Lead) pays affiliates for every lead they generate, regardless of whether the lead converts into a customer or not. Revenue Share market pays affiliates a percentage of the revenue generated by the customers they refer, for the lifetime of those customers' trading activity.</p></p>
