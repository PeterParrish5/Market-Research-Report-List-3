<p><p>The Thyristors & Triacs market consists of various types, including Silicon Controlled Rectifiers (SCR), Gate Turn-Off Thyristors (GTO), Integrated Gate-Commutated Thyristors (IGCTs), and others. SCRs are widely used in power control applications due to their high voltage handling capacity. GTOs provide more precise control of power and are used in high-frequency power converters. IGCTs combine the features of both SCR and GTO, making them suitable for various power transmission systems. Other types of thyristors and triacs include MCTs, SIThys, and Asymmetrical Thyristors, offering different performance characteristics for specific applications.</p></p>
