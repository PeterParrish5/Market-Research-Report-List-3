<p><p>Crossply OTR Tires come in two types based on their construction - Inner Tube and Tubeless. Inner Tube OTR tires have a separate tube inside the tire that holds the air pressure, while Tubeless OTR tires have an airtight seal between the tire and wheel rim, eliminating the need for a tube. Inner Tube tires are more common in older machinery, while Tubeless tires are preferred for their lower maintenance and reduced risk of punctures. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in the OTR tire market.</p></p>
