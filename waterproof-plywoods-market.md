<p><p>Waterproof plywoods are categorized based on their thickness into three main market types: Below 6mm, 6mm-18mm, and Above 18mm. Below 6mm plywoods are generally used in applications where weight and flexibility are important, such as in the manufacturing of furniture. Plywoods in the 6mm-18mm range are commonly used in construction and woodworking projects due to their strength and durability. Above 18mm plywoods are thicker and are typically used in heavy-duty applications like boat building or for flooring in high traffic areas.</p></p>
